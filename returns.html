Return Conditions

✓ Items We Accept

  • • Items in original condition and packaging
  • • Unused items with all tags attached
  • • Electronics in original packaging
  • • Clothing and accessories
  • • Home goods and decor

✗ Items We Cannot Accept

  • • Items without original packaging
  • • Used or damaged items
  • • Personalized or custom items
  • • Perishable goods
  • • Items returned after 30 days

How to Return an Item

1

Initiate Return

Log into your account and go to "My Orders" or contact our customer service team. We'll provide you with a prepaid return label.

2

Package Your Item

Securely package the item in its original packaging. Include all accessories, manuals, and documentation that came with the product.

3

Ship Using Our Label

Print and affix the prepaid return label to your package. Drop it off at any authorized shipping location.

4

Receive Refund

Once we receive and inspect your return, we'll process your refund within 5-7 business days to your original payment method.

Refund Information

Refund Timeline

Refund Methods

Original payment method
Store credit (if requested)
Exchange for different item
Different size/color (if available)

Special Circumstances

Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective item, please contact us immediately with photos of the damage. We'll arrange for a replacement or full refund at no cost to you.

Wrong Item Received

If you received the wrong item, please contact us within 7 days of delivery. We'll provide a prepaid return label and send the correct item immediately.

Final Sale Items

Items marked as "Final Sale" cannot be returned or exchanged unless they are defective or damaged upon arrival.

International Returns

International customers are responsible for return shipping costs. We recommend using a trackable shipping service for returns.

Need Help with Your Return?

Our customer service team is here to assist you with any questions about returns, exchanges, or refunds.
